Retrieval-Augmented Generation combines the power of large language models with external knowledge retrieval. Instead of relying solely on what an LLM learned during training, RAG systems fetch relevant information from document stores, databases, or knowledge bases to provide more accurate, up-to-date, and contextually relevant responses.
